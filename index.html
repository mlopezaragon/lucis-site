Features

Turn on, off, and adjust the brightness of your WiFi LED lights in one click. Colors, effects, saved scenes, scheduled timers, and system triggers. Global keyboard shortcut and Siri support. Compatible with Magic Home, MagicHue, and LEDENET controllers.

Available on the Mac App Store.

Funcionalidades

Encender, apagar y ajustar el brillo de tus luces LED WiFi con un clic. Colores, efectos, escenas guardadas, horarios programados y activadores del sistema. Atajo de teclado global y compatibilidad con Siri. Compatible con Magic Home, MagicHue y controladores LEDENET.

Disponible en la Mac App Store.
