The missing Mac app for your LED strips

Magic Home, MagicHue and LEDENET WiFi LED controllers only come with mobile apps. While there are over 20 apps on iOS and Android for these controllers, Lucis is the first and only native macOS option for controlling your lights without reaching for your phone.

Lucis is a native macOS application built with 26 Swift source files and zero external dependencies — no Electron, no web wrappers, no CocoaPods. It communicates directly with your LED controllers over your local WiFi network using raw TCP commands on port 5577. No internet, no cloud servers, no accounts. Your data never leaves your Mac.
