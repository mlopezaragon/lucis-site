Privacy Policy

Política de privacidad

Lucis - LED Control · April 2026

No data collection

Lucis does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app operates entirely on your local network and communicates directly with your LED devices over WiFi (TCP/UDP). No information ever leaves your network.

What Lucis accesses

  • Local network — Lucis sends TCP commands to your LED controllers on port 5577 and uses UDP broadcast on port 48899 for device discovery. All communication is local.
  • UserDefaults — Your device configurations, scenes, schedules, and preferences are stored locally on your Mac. This data never leaves your device.

No analytics, no tracking

Lucis contains no analytics frameworks, no crash reporting services, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party code. The app is built entirely with Apple's native frameworks.

No cloud, no accounts

Lucis does not require an account, login, or internet connection. It works offline as long as your Mac and your LED devices are on the same local network.

Children's privacy

Lucis does not collect any data from anyone, including children. The app is rated 4+ and contains no objectionable content.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL. Since Lucis collects no data, changes are unlikely.
